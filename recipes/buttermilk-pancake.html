Buttermilk Pancake

Homemade Buttermilk Pancakes

Be ready to prepare some light, fluffy, moist and delicious pancakes at home!

Ingredients

  • Flour: All-purpose flour will give your pancakes its structure and chewiness.
  • Sugar: 3 Tablespoons of white sugar should be enough.
  • Leaveners: Baking powder and baking soda makes your pancakes fluffly.
  • Salt: A small amount of salt to keep them from taste bland!
  • Buttermilk: It's in the name!, 3 whole cups is all you need.
  • Milk: Wholemilk gives the perfect batter consistency and cuts acidity.
  • Eggs: 3 whole eggs to add structure and rich flavor.
  • Butter: Melted Butter on top keep them nice and moist.

Instructions

  1. Mix the dry ingredients

    Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a large bowl.

  2. Mix the wet ingredients

    Beat the buttermilk, milk, eggs and melted butter together in another bowl.

  3. Make the pancakes

    Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix with a spoon or fork until the mixture are just-blended. Scoop the batter onto a hot, oiled griddle and cook until the pancake is bubbly on the top. Flip with a spatula and cook until both sides are brown.
