How to make Cookies

Here you will find step by step instructions on how to bake some delicious Cookies with the ingredients
list I will provide!

Ingredients

  • Flour: Any all-purpose flour.
  • Leaveners: Baking soda and Baking powder help the Cookies rise.
  • Butter: Two sticks of butter.
  • Sugar: We're baking some tasty Sugar Cookies!
  • Vanilla: Add some flavor to our Cookies!
  • Egg: It helps bind the Cookie dough together.

Instructions

  1. Beat sugar, butter, and vanilla in one bowl.
  2. Mix flour and leaveners in another bowl.
  3. Gradually blend the flour mixture into the butter mixture.
  4. Roll the dough into balls and bake until the edges are golden.
